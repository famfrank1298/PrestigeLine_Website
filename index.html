ODPhi Presents:
Spring '22 Mu "Prestige" Line

The purpose of this Brotherhood, a service/social fraternity dedicated to the needs and concerns of the community, shall be to promote and maintain the traditional values of Unity, Honesty, Integrity, and Leadership. This Brotherhood was founded in order to provide, to ANY man, a diverse fraternal experience which coincides with a higher education.

— What We Do —

Service Events

ODPhi absolutely values community service. We volunteer for the betterment of ourselves and our community. With each volunteering experience we develop deeper connections to those in our community, a better understanding of what we can do to become strong advocates, and strong leadership qualities that we can take everywhere we go.

— What's Next —

Future Partnering Organizations

We are constantly trying to branch out and help any and everywhere we can. We are currently working with the Forest Preserve District, Care For Real Organization, Man of Harbor, and of course Greater Chicago Food Depository. We hope to continuing partner with man more organizations to gain all sorts of skills.

Service Socials
